# Privacy Policy for Thyroid Nodule Management

**Effective Date: February 03, 2026**

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how the Thyroid Nodule Management App ("the App") handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed as a local clinical reference tool.

* **Personal Data**: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from your device.
* **Medical Data**: Any clinical data (TSH levels, nodule characteristics, etc.) entered into the App is processed locally on your device to provide recommendations. This data is **not** uploaded to any servers and is cleared when the session is closed.
* **Third-Party Access**: We do not share any data with third parties.

## 2. Permissions

The App may require basic permissions (like network access for updates) but it does not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

## 3. Medical Disclaimer

The App is intended for use by healthcare professionals as a reference tool based on established clinical guidelines. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical judgment, diagnosis, or treatment.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
